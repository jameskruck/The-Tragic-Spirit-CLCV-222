Course Schedule

Below is the tentative schedule for the course. Readings and assignments are subject to change based on class discussions and needs.

Week 1: Introduction to Greek Tragedy

  • Understanding tragic form and context
  • Critical approaches to ancient performance
  • Readings: Selections from Nicole Loraux, "Tragic Ways of Killing a Woman"

Week 2: Justice and Gender in the Ancient World

  • Tragic perspectives on justice
  • Women’s voices in ancient drama
  • Readings: Froma Zeitlin, "Playing the Other"

Week 3: The Oresteia - Cycles of Violence

  • Family obligations across cultures
  • Maternal power and resistance
  • Readings: Aeschylus, Agamemnon

Further weeks will be added soon, covering topics like Oedipus, Antigone, Medea, and Bacchae.
