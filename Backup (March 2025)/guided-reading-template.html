CLCV 222

Guided Reading Assignment

Expected Learning Outcomes

After completing this guided reading, you will be able to:

  • Analyze classical texts through close reading and critical interpretation
  • Identify key themes and concepts in relation to cultural contexts
  • Develop connections between ancient texts and contemporary discussions
  • Articulate your own cultural perspective in relation to the text

Purpose

This guided reading process develops critical skills that extend beyond the classroom:

  • Analytical reading strategies applicable to any complex text
  • Cultural awareness and comparative analysis abilities
  • Self-directed learning and metacognitive skills
  • Evidence-based argumentation and interpretation

Knowledge:

  • Understanding of classical tragic themes and forms
  • Recognition of cultural contexts and influences
  • Awareness of diverse interpretive approaches

Skills:

  • Close reading and textual analysis
  • Cross-cultural comparison and interpretation
  • Critical reflection and self-assessment
